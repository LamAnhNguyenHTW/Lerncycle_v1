# Initial Concept

An AI learning app that allows the users to manage file (create semester folder, subject folder and week folder), to upload pdf and take notes, to have an ai chatbot to chat with (context of the uploaded files) and research for external resources, feynman technique have ai asking questions to verify your knowledge and understanding, three modes (professor, fellow student and 5-year-old child) and flashcards with active recall

# Product Definition: Lerncycle AI Learning App

## Vision
Lerncycle is an AI-powered learning companion designed to help students and lifelong learners maximize their learning efficiency and knowledge retention through structured organization and advanced active learning techniques.

## Core Goals
*   **Enhanced Active Learning:** Leverage AI to facilitate deep understanding using the Feynman technique and active recall.
*   **Structured Organization:** Provide a logical, hierarchical file management system (Semester > Subject > Week) for study materials.
*   **AI-Powered Assistance:** Offer instant, context-aware support through an AI chatbot that understands uploaded PDFs and performs external research.

## Target Audience
*   **Students & Lifelong Learners:** Individuals seeking to improve their academic performance and master complex subjects through proven learning methodologies.

## Platform
*   **Web Application:** A responsive and accessible platform optimized for structured study and research.

## Key Features
*   **File Management:** Hierarchical folder structure for semesters, subjects, and weeks.
*   **PDF Integration:** Upload and manage PDF documents as primary study sources.
*   **Contextual AI Chatbot:** Chat with an AI that has full context of uploaded PDFs and the ability to research external resources.
*   **Adaptive AI Personalities:** Switch between 'Professor', 'Fellow Student', and '5-Year-Old Child' modes for tailored explanations.
*   **Feynman Technique Testing:** AI-led interactive sessions where the AI asks probing questions to verify the user's knowledge and understanding.
*   **Note-Taking & Flashcards:** Integrated note-taking with AI-assisted flashcard generation for active recall.
