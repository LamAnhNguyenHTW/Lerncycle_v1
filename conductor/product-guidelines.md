# Product Guidelines: Lerncycle

## Overview
Lerncycle's design and user experience are centered on minimizing cognitive load and maximizing learning outcomes through a clean, distraction-free interface and supportive AI interactions.

## Design Principles
*   **Minimalist & Modern (Notion-Style):** Prioritize white space, high-quality typography, and a clean, hierarchical layout. Use subtle borders and soft shadows to define sections without clutter.
*   **Focus-Centric Design:** Present only the most relevant information and controls for the current task. Use progressive disclosure to reveal advanced features only when needed.
*   **Clarity & Directness:** All application text and AI responses should be clear, concise, and easy to understand. Avoid unnecessary jargon or overly complex explanations.

## UX & Interaction
*   **Balanced AI Partnership:** The AI should act as a supportive partner, offering proactive suggestions for flashcards, Feynman questions, or next steps, while always respecting user autonomy and providing clear paths for manual control.
*   **Contextual Assistance:** AI interactions should be deeply integrated into the learning workflow, providing help that is relevant to the currently viewed material (e.g., specific PDF page, note section).
*   **Interactive Feedback:** Provide immediate and clear feedback for all user actions, such as successful file uploads, flashcard creation, or AI session progress.

## Voice & Tone
*   **Clear & Direct:** The primary tone is professional yet accessible. Communication should be focused on providing the user with the information they need as efficiently as possible.
*   **Helpful & Encouraging:** While remaining direct, the AI should maintain a supportive tone that encourages the user to engage with the material and continue their learning journey.

## Visual Identity
*   **Minimalist Palette:** Use a primarily monochrome or limited color palette (e.g., shades of gray, white, and a single accent color) to maintain a clean and focused aesthetic.
*   **High Readability:** Prioritize high-contrast text and generous line spacing to ensure all study materials and AI explanations are easy to read on any screen.
